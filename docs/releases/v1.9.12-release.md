## Summary

**v1.9.12** ships Conduit Mesh through **C14** (HTTP pull, mesh-native DM wire, long-poll, SSE stream, Tor SOCKS L1), desktop dual-window soaks for **WS (C7)**, **HTTP (C10)**, and **SSE (C14)**, plus trust/link/export confirmation UX and voice-note player work landed on `main`.

**Chat → Save to Vault** remains **disabled** (deferred to [v1.9.13](../program/v1.9.13-scope.md)). Community feature patches remain **PAUSED**.
