## Summary

**v1.9.13** finishes the desktop **Local Encrypted Store (LES)** as the Vault data plane: ciphertext at rest, one catalog for Secure Upload and chat Save, stable previews, and Remove from Vault.

Tag **`v1.9.13` first**, then proceed to **`v1.9.14`** (pool + transport presets).

Community feature patches remain **PAUSED** (ACC-02).
