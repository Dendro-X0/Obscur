## Release-Blocking vs Non-Blocking Policy

1. Blocking for v0.8.9:
   - `pnpm -C apps/pwa exec tsc --noEmit --pretty false`
   - Focused reliability/runtime/profile/storage tests
   - `pnpm release:preflight`
2. Non-blocking for v0.8.9:
   - Remaining unstable legacy Vitest suites with known expectation drift or environment sensitivity.
